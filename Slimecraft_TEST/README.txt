                      Monomi Park please don't sue me.

            This pack aims to find a nice middle ground between
                the worlds of Slime Rancher and Minecraft.

           The hope is that by navigating this fine line the pack
          can offer some wiggly goodness while still being useable
           with other texture packs or mods, which may have their
                 assets based on default Minecraft textures.

                                -sushimus
